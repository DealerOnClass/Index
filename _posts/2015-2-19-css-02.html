Units

Technically, a unit is a quantity chosen to represent length. Within CSS we have several options available but we must intelligently choose the proper unit for the job considering the immediate function and context.

px

Relative to the physical pixel on screen.

pt

1 pt is 1/72 of an inch, print unit, DO NOT USE.

em

Relative to the font-size of the element. 1em means 1 times the size of the current font.

rem

Relative to the font-size of the root element.

%

Relative to containing element.
